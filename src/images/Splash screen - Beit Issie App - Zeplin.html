    What is an "active" project?
    
                    
    
                        Active projects are the projects you are currently working on. If youre done working on a project, you can archive it and it wont be counted against your total. Technically you could have hundreds of archived projects and still fit on the $29/mo plan as long as you have no more than 12 active projects.
                    
    
                
    
                
    
                    
    How do Starter and Growing Business plans work?
    
                    
    
                        In Starter and Growing Business plans, you can invite as many collaborators as you want to your projects, but only the subscribed account can create projects. Collaborators or clients can access projects that theyre invited to and upload designs.
                    
    
                
    
                
    
                    
    How does the Organization plan work?
    
                    
    
                        In the Organization plan, youll have access to a team dashboard where you can assign roles to define who can create projects, edit and access resources. Organization members can browse and join organization projects, without an invitation.
                    
    
                    
    
                        Your organization members will be your designers, developers and other product-related colleagues that need access to resources in Zeplin. Clients can be invited separately to projects, without additional cost.
